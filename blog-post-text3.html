BEAUTICIAN TRAINING CAMP AT BELLARY

Beautician training camp was held at Bellary on 31st of August 2020. It was attended by over 75 enthusiastic women between 18-45 years of age spread across districts in Karnataka. It was a 6 hour training on beauty parlor management conducted in the premises. It was graced by eminent women entrepreneurs excelling in the same field who shared their valuable experience and knowledge with the trainees. The camp provided a valuable insight to various training related queries.
